Freestyle Society

Mission

Freestyle Society is non-profit organization that is actively spreading the art of breaking to as many people as possible. Breaking, also known as Break-dancing, is a form of freestyle hiphop dancing that originated from New York city; The Bronx. From there it has spread accorss the globe through performances, competitions, and social media.

Our mission is to empower the youth of Edmonton by teaching classes, hosting performances & compeitions, and providing a safe place for freestyle dancers to practice and hang out

Objectives

Teach Classes

In order to spread breaking to as many people as possible, we are offering our level 1 classes for free. We focus heavily on foundation. Whether it's ballet, jazz, or breaking, all dance styles have their foundation.

Breaking foundation can be broken into roughly four main categories: toprock, footwork, freezes, and powermoves. These classes will equip you with the moves necassary to move onto higher levels.

Host Events

A Bgirl or Bboy's true test is on the dance floor. For those breakers that want to comepete we want to provide opportunities for them to test their skills against others. Whether it be in a breaking battle, or choreographed showcase competition, dancers can inspire others to work harder and improve.

Session Spots

Our long term goal is to have our very own location with convenient access to public transportation and large space for dancers of all ages to use.

We understand how it feels to want to practice but not have a space to do so. Either the place is too expensive, takes too long to get to, or just doesn't fit with our schedule.

Imagine that one day we will have a session spot that's affordable, conveniently located, and open all-day-long just for breakers to practice!
