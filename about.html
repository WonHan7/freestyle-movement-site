About Us

Our Mission

Freestyle Movement is a registered non-profit company that supports Edmonton breakers, bboys and bgirls (commonly known as break dancers). We provide regular practice sessions, classes and workshops, as well as competition style events. We hope to see the Breaking community grow in size and in skill as people from different backgrounds come together and experience the culture of Hip Hop.

Our Story

Every dream has a first step, and for us this is it. From ideas ranging from dance studio cafes, to having large open warehouse space for competitions, we at Freestyle Movement wanted to make an impact. United behind an idea, we took the first step to begin formalizing our Friday night practice sessions, teaching Hip Hop and Breaking workshops, and beginning to host dance competitions.
