Foundation Expansion - Bboy Link

Bboy Link’s style of dance focuses on clean calculated movements matching the rhythm of the percussion and the accents in the melody. Musicality is attained through mastery of the foundation movements to be able to execute any move at any time. Having a balance of power moves, style moves, and freezes, Link’s style is a jack of all trades, but master of none.
