Welcome to Freestyle Society

Welcome to Freestyle Society, we are Edmonton's drop-in breaking a.k.a breakdance location. We provide bboys and bgirls with a place to practice every Friday night from 7:00 - 10:30. We also offer drop-in & registered classes. As a non-profit organization, we aim to provide three main services to the breaking community: teaching dance, hosting events, and providing a safe and fun place for dancers to hang-out & practice.

We hope to educate people about the breaking art form, and Hiphop culture so that we can build an appreciation for our dance and strengthen the local dance community.
