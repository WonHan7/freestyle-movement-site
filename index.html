--- title: Freestyle Movement | Edmonton Breakdance and Hiphop Community ---

Join the movement

Freestyle Movement creates opportunities for bboys, bgirls, choreographers and freestyle dancers to grow and build the Hiphop community in Edmonton. We teach classes, organize competitions and run a weekly open practice session.

Classes

We teach breaking (Break Dance), freestyle Hiphop, grooves and choreography.

Weekly Practice

Every Friday night we invite dancers to practice and cypher. All ages and skill-levels welcome.

Events & Competitions

Throughout the year we work together with university and high school dance clubs to put together dance showcases and competitions.
