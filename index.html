Dance Workshops

Once in a while we try to spice things up a bit and invite different dancers to share a little bit of their experience. They teach workshops on Friday nights to help dancers get a taste of other dance styles like House, Popping, and Locking or focus in on improving core aspects of Breakin, like power-move workshops.

Battles & Events

The best place for Bboys & Bgirls to challenge themselves is in the Cypher. But for those dancers that want to take things more seriously we are hosting tournament style competitions periodically throughout the year for competitive dancers to win some cash from their hard training.
