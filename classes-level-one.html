Level One - Foundation Basics

This level is open to all ages and skill levels. It is meant to be an introductory class to allow people to experience the raw nature of breaking. This level consists of the fundamental elements of breaking: toprock, footwork, freeze and power. Foundation Basics is taught in a 4 week cycle, in which dancers are able to join in any week within the cycle.

After the 4 week classes, dancers will be able to enter a bboy cypher. Each cypher round should showcase unique combinations of the moves learned in class or from other sources.

Foundation Basics 4-Week Curriculum

Week 1

  • Indian step
  • Salsa step
  • Capoeira step
  • Kneedrop
  • 6-step
  • Shuffles
  • Knee freeze
  • Bboy Stance

Week 2

  • Jump steps
  • Power step
  • Sweep
  • Kickouts
  • 3-step
  • Baby freeze
  • Hand Glide
  • Buttspin

Week 3

  • Running-man kicks
  • Standing spins
  • Corkscrew
  • CC
  • Crossover-step
  • Shoulder freeze
  • Backspin
  • Zip finish

Week 4

  • Kicking steps
  • Brooklyn Uprock Drop
  • Flare Entry
  • Zulu spin
  • Helicopter
  • Kneespin
  • Handstand
  • Headstand
